Abstract Submission

Abstract Submission Deadline : August 15,2023

Sessions (Topics)
No. Sessions Scope
SS01 Materials for Energy Luminescent materials, plasmonic materials, piezoelectric materials, biofuels, magnetic materials, semiconductors, spintronic materials, biomaterials, liquid crystals, MXenes, Energy Harvesting Materials
SS02 Fuel Cells, Solar and other energy devices Semiconductor devices, power devices, solar cells, supercapacitors, fuel cells
SS03 Advancement with rechargeable batteries Rechargeable batteries, solid state batteries, thin film batteries, flexible batteries
SS04 Synchrotron techniques for energy materials and batteries X-ray spectroscopy, X-ray imaging, advanced synchrotron tools for battery and energy material characterisation
SS05 Computational methods for energy related research
SS06 Environmental Issues of batteries and energy related material

Guideline for Abstract Submission

→ Abstract of 300- 500 words highlighting the key results should be sent to abs.icemrb2023@mru.edu.in. Authors are suggested to use attached format.Download file here.

→Please name your file name as session number_presentation type_Authorsurname.
In case your submitting your abstract for presentation in session 1, your mode of presentation is oral and your surname is Singh, your filename will be SS1_OP_Singh. In case of poster presentation mode, your file name will be SS1_PP_Singh


→ Please note that conference will take place at university campus in offline mode. Also your actual mode of presentation will be decided by the conference committee.
